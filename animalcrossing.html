About ACNH

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is one of the Animal Crossing series. It is a life simulation and education simulation genre or I would categorize it as a casual game. It was released on 20 March, 2020. In this game, we could create and decorate our own dream island!

Seasons

The seasons in ACNH keep changing just like the real time. There are four seasons which is spring, summer, fall, and winter. From the video above, it is currently fall since it was taken in November. How to know it is already fall? As you can see, the tree's leaves are red which shows the sign of fall. If it was summer, the leaves will be bright green. I like how ACNH include seasonal theme, since in Malaysia, we do not have seasons like this. It made me happy when I am able to "feel" how to be in those seasons.

ACNH:Various event

Just like the seasonal themes, ACNH also include events around the world such as Halloween, Thanksgiving, Bunny Day, etc. Usually, all these events requires player to collect items in order to actually get a Do It Yourself (DIY) recipes. These recipes is used to create our own furnitures. As I said, ACNH is a simulation game. So, player will be able to decorate their own island to their heart content. As for me, I am not too diligent to decorate my island since I am not that creative but I do enjoy talking to my villagers. (p/s: villagers are the animals that are also live on the island with you)

Museum's section

In ACNH, there are shops and a museum. In my opinion, I think that museum is my favorite place to be because the interior of it is so pleasing to my eyes. The vibes I got in there are exactly the same as the real museum. Even though I do not like the process of collecting the bugs, fishes, fossils, and arts to finish the collections of museum, I will still come to the museum to refresh my mind. I like how pretty the aquarium is and the detail of the bugs and fishes are accurate and fascinating. Overall, I would recommend this game to those who wants to relieve their stress because ACNH is made for a laid-back and casual time.
